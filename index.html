# Kai – Privacy Policy **Last updated:** December 17, 2025 --- ## 1. Overview Kai is a conversational experience designed to support reflection, grounding, and truth-telling. Kai does not collect, store, or share personal information beyond what is required for the chat’s basic operation within OpenAI’s platform. --- ## 2. Data Collection and Use - **No external data storage:** Conversations and reflections exist only within your current OpenAI session. - **No analytics or tracking:** Kai does not use cookies, analytics tools, or third-party tracking. - **User control:** You may delete any saved reflections or memory within the chat at any time using the provided commands. - **Session data:** OpenAI may retain anonymized session data as part of its platform operations, governed by [OpenAI’s Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy). Kai itself does not access or store that data independently. --- ## 3. Shared Responsibility Kai is not a medical or therapeutic service. If you share sensitive information, it remains between you and the OpenAI platform. You are responsible for managing your own privacy and ensuring no personally identifying details are included unless you choose to do so. --- ## 4. Contact For questions or concerns about Kai’s privacy policy, please contact the creator through the GitHub repository or the platform where Kai is hosted. ---
